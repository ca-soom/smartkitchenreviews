Tips for Getting the Best Results from a Budget Air Fryer

  1. Don't overcrowd the basket — Air needs to circulate. Cook in batches if needed.
  2. Shake halfway through — Budget models have less powerful fans, so manual shaking is important.
  3. Preheat for 3 minutes — Most budget models don't have preheat functions, so run it empty for 3 minutes.
  4. Use a little oil spray — A light mist of cooking spray makes food crispier.
  5. Reduce temperature by 10-15°F — Budget models often run hotter than the display shows.
