Cooking Performance

French Fries:

  • Ninja AF101: Crispy and even. 15 minutes at 400°F.
  • Cosori Pro LE: Equally crispy, slightly larger batch. 14 minutes at 400°F.
  • Tie — Both produce excellent fries.

Chicken Wings:

  • Ninja AF101: Perfectly crispy skin. 20 minutes at 380°F.
  • Cosori Pro LE: Very good but slightly less even. 22 minutes at 380°F.
  • Ninja wins — Slightly better air circulation.

Vegetables:

  • Ninja AF101: Good browning. 12 minutes at 375°F.
  • Cosori Pro LE: Better due to shake reminder. 12 minutes at 375°F.
  • Cosori wins — Shake reminder ensures even cooking.

Build Quality and Durability

Ninja:

  • Basket coating is thicker and more durable
  • Housing feels solid and premium
  • Handles are sturdy and well-designed
  • Typical lifespan: 3-5 years

Cosori:

  • Basket coating is good but can wear after 18-24 months
  • Housing is decent but lighter
  • Touchscreen is responsive
  • Typical lifespan: 2-4 years

Winner: Ninja — Better long-term durability.

Smart Features

Ninja:

  • No app connectivity
  • Basic digital controls
  • Simple and reliable

Cosori:

  • VeSync app with 100+ recipes
  • Remote monitoring (some models)
  • Firmware updates
  • Voice control with Alexa/Google (some models)

Winner: Cosori — If you value smart features.

Noise Level

The Recall Issue

In 2023, Cosori recalled 2 million air fryers due to a potential fire hazard. The issue was a wire connection that could overheat. Cosori handled the recall professionally, offering free replacements to all affected customers.

This is worth noting, but it shouldn't be a dealbreaker. Every brand has occasional recalls. Cosori's response was responsible and transparent.
