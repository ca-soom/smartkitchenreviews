Features That Matter for Families

Dual Baskets

The single biggest upgrade for families. Being able to cook two foods simultaneously cuts your cooking time in half. Worth the extra $30-50.

Large Capacity

Don't buy anything under 5 quarts for a family of 4. You'll end up cooking in batches, which defeats the purpose.

Easy Cleanup

With a family, you'll use the air fryer daily. Look for dishwasher-safe baskets and non-stick coatings that actually last.

Presets

One-touch presets are a lifesaver when you're juggling kids and cooking. Look for models with presets for the foods your family eats most.
