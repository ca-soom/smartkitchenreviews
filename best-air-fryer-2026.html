How We Test Air Fryers

We put every air fryer through the same rigorous tests:

  1. French Fry Test — 1 pound of frozen fries at 400°F. We measure crispiness, evenness, and texture.
  2. Chicken Wing Test — Fresh wings at 380°F. We evaluate skin crispiness and meat juiciness.
  3. Vegetable Test — Broccoli and Brussels sprouts at 375°F. We check for even browning.
  4. Reheat Test — Leftover pizza at 350°F. We compare to microwave reheating.
  5. Noise Test — We measure decibel levels during operation.
  6. Ease of Cleaning — We evaluate basket coating, dishwasher safety, and residue removal.

What to Look for When Buying an Air Fryer

Capacity

  • 1-2 quarts: Single servings only. Good for dorm rooms.
  • 3-4 quarts: Couples or small families (2-3 people).
  • 5-6 quarts: Medium families (3-5 people).
  • 7+ quarts: Large families or meal prepping.

Features Worth Paying For

  • Adjustable temperature — Essential. Don't buy one without it.
  • Digital controls — More precise than dials.
  • Shake reminder — Ensures even cooking.
  • Dual baskets — Game-changer for families.
  • Dehydrate function — Nice to have for jerky and dried fruit.

Features You Don't Need

  • WiFi connectivity — Gimmicky. You're standing right next to it.
  • 100+ presets — You'll use 3-4 of them max.
  • Built-in cameras — Yes, these exist. No, you don't need one.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are air fryers worth it?

Yes. Air fryers use 70-80% less oil than deep frying while producing similar crispy results. They also preheat faster than ovens and use less energy.

Can you put foil in an air fryer?

Yes, but don't block the air vents. Use small pieces of foil to wrap food or line the bottom of the basket. Never cover the entire basket.

How long do air fryers last?

A quality air fryer should last 3-5 years with regular use. The basket coating is usually the first thing to wear out.

Do air fryers use a lot of electricity?

No. Most air fryers use 1,000-1,500 watts, which is less than a standard oven. A typical air fryer session costs about $0.10-0.15 in electricity.
